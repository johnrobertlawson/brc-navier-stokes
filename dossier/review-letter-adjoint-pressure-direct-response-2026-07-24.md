## Claimed advance

For the reviewed terminal adjoint layer, write

\[
w=a-\varphi=q+r,
\]

where

\[
q(\tau)
=
\int_0^\tau e^{\nu(\tau-s)\Delta}
\left[
\nu\Delta\varphi+
\mathbb P(b(s)\cdot\nabla\varphi)
\right]\,ds
\]

is the direct heat response and

\[
\partial_\tau r-\nu\Delta r-\mathbb P(b\cdot\nabla r)
=
\mathbb P(b\cdot\nabla q),
\qquad r(0)=0.
\]

The note claims that the detector product

\[
g_i=b_k\partial_k\varphi_i
\]

has a uniform first moment and zero mean. The heat--Leray kernel should
therefore have a dipole rather than monopole far field, giving

\[
\|q(\tau)\|_{L^\infty(Q_k)}
\lesssim
\tau^{1/4}\langle k\rangle^{-N}
+
\tau\langle k\rangle^{-4}.
\]

Combining this with the previously reviewed centre-uniform local-energy
bound and unit-cube \(\mathcal H^1\) decomposition gives

\[
\int_0^h
\|\nabla\pi^*_{[q,b]}\|_1\,d\tau
\lesssim
h^{5/4}D_b(h)^{1/3}+h^{1/6},
\qquad
D_b(h)=\int_0^h\|\nabla b\|_2^2\,d\tau.
\]

Since the reviewed input already forces \(D_b(h)\gtrsim h^{-3}\), a
fixed direct-response pressure fraction would force

\[
D_b(h)\gtrsim h^{-15/4}.
\]

Otherwise a fixed pressure fraction survives in the zero-data,
drift-feedback remainder \(r\). This is a dichotomy only; neither branch
is excluded.

## Audit questions

Please identify the first invalid implication rather than repairing it
silently.

1. **Exact split.** Are the equations for \(q\) and \(r\), their signs,
   and their Leray projections correct for
   \(w=a-\varphi\)?
2. **Weighted endpoint products.** Does
   \(b\in L^{3,\infty}\), with fixed Schwartz \(\varphi\), uniformly give
   \[
   \|\langle x\rangle^N
     b\cdot\nabla\varphi\|_2\lesssim1,
   \qquad
   \|\langle x\rangle
     b\cdot\nabla\varphi\|_1\lesssim1
   \]
   through the stated Lorentz products?
3. **Cancellation.** Is
   \(\int b_k\partial_k\varphi_i=0\) justified at the weak-\(L^3\)
   endpoint, and is componentwise zero mean the correct cancellation
   for the heat--Leray kernel?
4. **Dipole heat--Leray bound.** Check the near/far decomposition,
   uniformly as the heat time tends to zero. Does it really yield, for
   \(N\ge8\),
   \[
   \|q(\tau)\|_{L^\infty(Q_k)}
   \lesssim
   \tau^{1/4}\langle k\rangle^{-N}
   +\tau\langle k\rangle^{-4}?
   \]
   In particular, check that the local heat part, the nonlocal Leray
   part, and the fixed \(\nu\Delta\varphi\) term all obey this same
   estimate without an omitted negative power of \(\tau\).
5. **Dual pressure source.** Is the pressure generated by the adjoint
   entry \(q\) representable as
   \[
   \nabla\Delta^{-1}\operatorname{div}((q\cdot\nabla)b),
   \]
   with every vector component of \((q\cdot\nabla)b\) having zero
   integral?
6. **Weighted Hardy cubes.** Does the previously reviewed unit-cube
   \(\mathcal H^1\) molecular estimate apply to this noncompact \(q\)?
   Does it give
   \[
   \|\nabla\pi^*_{[q,b]}(\tau)\|_1
   \lesssim
   \sum_k
   \langle k\rangle
   \|q(\tau)\|_{L^\infty(Q_k)}
   \|\nabla b(\tau)\|_{L^2(Q_k)}
   \]
   with a finite right-hand side and no hidden global-energy constant?
7. **Inner cubes.** From the preceding bound and the reviewed local
   dissipation estimate, are
   \[
   \sum_{|k|\le R}c_k^2\lesssim\tau^{1/2}R^5,
   \qquad
   \int_0^h\sum_{|k|\le R}c_ke_k
   \lesssim h^{3/4}R^3
   \]
   correct, including the condition \(h\lesssim R^2\)?
8. **Outer cubes.** Is the lattice-shell computation
   \[
   \left(\sum_{|k|>R}c_k^2\right)^{1/2}
   \lesssim
   \tau R^{-3/2}+\tau^{1/4}R^{-11/2}
   \]
   correct, and do the two time Cauchy--Schwarz estimates give precisely
   the powers in equation (28)?
9. **Optimisation.** Does
   \[
   R\asymp(h^{3/4}\sqrt{D_b})^{2/9}
   \]
   balance the first two terms, and does the reviewed inverse-cubic
   floor make the rapid-tail term \(O(h^{1/6})\)? Does this prove the
   asserted \(h^{5/4}D_b^{1/3}\) bound uniformly when \(D_b\) is much
   larger than its floor?
10. **Dichotomy.** Does linearity of pressure in the adjoint entry and
    the triangle inequality rigorously give the exhaustive alternative
    \(D_b(h)\gtrsim h^{-15/4}\) or a fixed pressure floor for \(r\)?
11. **Remainder energy.** Does integration by parts in
    \(\langle\mathbb P(b\cdot\nabla q),r\rangle\), followed by
    \(L^{3,\infty}\times L^{6,2}\times L^2\), prove
    \[
    \sup_{\tau\le h}\|r(\tau)\|_2^2
    +\nu\int_0^h\|\nabla r\|_2^2\,d\tau
    \lesssim h^2?
    \]
12. **Extreme-branch ledger.** Check the claimed
    \(h^{-15/4}\) reversed-energy gain, \(o(h^{-15/4})\) escape radius,
    \(h^{-17/4}\) enstrophy heat cover, \(h^{-45/4}\) velocity-reservoir
    volume, \(h^{-51/4}\) heat-cell count, strict physical depth
    \(\sigma=o(h^{15/4})\), and ancestor clock \(h^{17/2}\).
13. **Claim boundary.** Does any step silently exclude the feedback
    branch, sum event costs, construct a rough endpoint adjoint, prove
    regularity, or settle a Clay alternative?
